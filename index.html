Let's get CSSing

Today I dived into CSS, particulary focusing on text formatting and style using tags such as "font-family" or "line-height". I can now make a text look bolder, or change it's color and background, underline it, remove the underline from links, change the font or align it.

12 of March

CSS Layout

I started using div and span tags to group either parts of text or block of elements; then I modified width and height of my div blocks and set the overflow value(visible, hidden or auto). Afterwards, I started applying margins(top bottom left or right), borders(px style and colours) and paddings to blocks and images.
Then I started using position attribute. While struggling with relative and absolute position, I immediately understood the use and the potential of fixed position.The google maps video was useful to understand the functioning of z index.
